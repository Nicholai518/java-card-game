# java-card-game

Basic Java program that produces the below output.

```
Nick has the below cards in their hand:
  Six of Diamonds
  Ten of Hearts
  Two of Clubs
  Three of Spades
  King of Diamonds

Tommy has the below cards in their hand:
  Seven of Clubs
  Five of Hearts
  Four of Clubs
  Two of Diamonds
  Queen of Spades
```

- The `solution` branch has implementations that satisfy all tests.
